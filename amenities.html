Amenities & Services

Everything you need for a perfect stay

Infinity Pool

Relax by our rooftop infinity pool, offering stunning panoramic views of the skyline. Enjoy poolside service with craft cocktails and light bites from our Sun Deck Bar. Open daily from 6:00 AM to 10:00 PM.

Fine Dining

Experience culinary excellence at 'The New Africa Table'. Our award-winning chefs prepare locally sourced, seasonal dishes inspired by Mediterranean flavors. We also offer 24-hour in-room dining for your convenience.

Serenity Spa

Rejuvenate your body and mind. Our full-service spa offers a range of treatments including Swedish massage, deep tissue therapy, facials, and body wraps. Includes sauna and steam room access for all guests.
